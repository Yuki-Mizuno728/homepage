Welcome to my home page!
I am a postdoctoral researcher at Utrecht University in the Netherlands in the research group of Pieter Belmans from February 2026. Previously, I was an assistant professor at Waseda University in Tokyo, Japan from October 2024 to January 2026. My CV is here.
My research interests lie in Algebraic geometry and related fields. In particular, I am interested in noncommutative algebraic geometry, moduli spaces of sheaves, derived categories, derived algebraic geometry, generalized complex geometry, Calabi-Yau manifolds and Mirror symmetry.
